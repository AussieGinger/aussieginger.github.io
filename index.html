Bat Monitoring at Lake Powell

Internship Project For Joshua Burns


Who am I and why did I do a website?

Hi. I'm Joshua Burns

I am currently studying a bachelor of Computer Science at Swinburne University, majoring in Cyber Security.

I thought I would do a website instead of just a powerpoint because I would often find myself saying something along the lines of:

"I am studying computer science, I know it doesn't neccesarily relate to environment ect."

So I thought I would show how my computer science studies can in fact assist me with environmental work.

Backstory for the Project

I was told something along the lines of:

"There might be bats, noone has even looked into this, you would be going in blind"

Initially I wasn't going to do this project. However I had a change of heart when I went on a trip to Vanuatu. I went into a cave filled with bats, and, well, was pretty struck by what a cool animal they are. Unfortunately I don't have a photo of the cave or bats within the cave, however, the noises of the bats rushing past me was surreal.
